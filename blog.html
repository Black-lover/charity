Child Help Foundation is India's leading NGO dedicated to improving the lives of children nationwide. With a focus on education, healthcare, nutrition, and shelter, we've been making a direct impact on marginalized children since 2010. You can support our cause by donating securely online, helping us provide essential services and life- saving treatments. Your contributions are tax-exempt under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, making a double impact: changing lives and saving on taxes. Join us in creating a brighter future for children by donating online today. Together, we can make a difference!
